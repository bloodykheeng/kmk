Our FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

How does KMK Cargo handle customs clearance for different types of goods?

KMK Cargo specializes in custom clearance solutions for all types of goods, whether they arrive by air or sea, in bags, cartons, on pallets, or in containers. We offer tailored services based on the specific needs of your consignment, ensuring that your goods clear customs efficiently and swiftly. Our experienced team is well-versed in the latest policies and notifications, ensuring compliance and smooth clearance for various categories of cargo.

Can KMK Cargo assist with the transportation and storage of goods after customs clearance?

Yes, KMK Cargo provides comprehensive logistics solutions beyond customs clearance. After your goods clear customs, we can move them to any destination across the country, offering storage, break bulk, and distribution services as needed. Our aim is to provide a seamless, end-to-end service that meets all your logistics requirements, ensuring safe and timely delivery of your goods.

What makes KMK Cargo different from other logistics and customs clearance service providers?

What sets KMK Cargo apart is our comprehensive, integrated approach to logistics and customs clearance, tailored to meet the specific needs of each client. Our expertise in the field since 2017, combined with our commitment to innovation and technology, such as E-Filing and online transactions with Customs, positions us as market leaders. Our experienced team provides personalized attention to each consignment and seeks creative solutions to meet challenges, ensuring a professional, efficient, and timely service tailored to your requirements.

How much do you charge for cargo?

The cost for transporting cargo with KMK Transport Wise Cargo Uganda Limited varies depending on several factors, including the type of goods, their weight, volume, destination, and specific service requirements such as storage or special handling. We recommend contacting us directly with your specific shipping details for an accurate quote.

How long does a cargo take?

The delivery time for cargo depends on the destination, the type of transportation used, and the specific service options chosen. KMK Transport is committed to providing timely and efficient delivery services tailored to meet customers' demands. For precise timing, please provide us with the details of your shipment.

What requirements are needed?

Shipping with KMK Transport requires details such as the nature and quantity of goods, preferred service (e.g., supply chain management, logistics, truck hire), destination, and any special handling or storage needs. For customs clearance and tax consultations, necessary documents might include invoices, packing lists, and any relevant permits or licenses. Contact us for a detailed list of requirements based on your specific cargo and services needed.
