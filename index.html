Warehousing and storage

KMK Cargo offers comprehensive warehousing services across the country, ensuring the safe and timely delivery of products. Their services are characterized by professional management and the provision of optimal storage conditions, including spacious, airy, and hygienic warehouses located strategically to facilitate easy transportation. They are equipped to handle large quantities of storage, catering to various storage needs with reliable solutions. The entire warehousing process, from storage to distribution, is managed by experienced and trained professionals, with thorough inspections conducted at both storing and retrieving phases to ensure the integrity of the consignment.

Transportation

Our expertise lies in understanding our customers' businesses, supply chains, and distribution networks to develop a comprehensive, integrated logistics strategy that provides all supply-related services through a single window. We boast an excellent track record in delivering unique and prompt transportation solutions, capable of handling any type of cargo nationwide. Our fleet of trailers and containerized trucks ensures the safe transport of goods across distances. Additionally, our logistics personnel closely monitor each shipment to guarantee its security and timely delivery to the designated destination. Our domestic transportation services include road movements to fulfill our customers' delivery commitments.

Custom clearance

As a leading specialist and licensed Custom House Agent since 2016, KMK Cargo stands out in the market for its expertise in customs clearance. We cater to businesses of all sizes, from importers to wholesalers and retailers, across all regions of Uganda. Our services are comprehensive, covering consignments arriving by air or sea, in any form—bags, cartons, pallets, or containers. KMK Cargo provides customized solutions for customs clearance, onward forwarding, storage, break-bulk, and distribution, tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. We thrive on challenges and strive to find creative solutions for our clients' requirements, aiming to deliver efficient, professional, and timely services. Our experienced team is well-versed in importation, taxation, and clearance procedures, ensuring swift and efficient customs clearance. We stay updated on policy changes and conduct most of our transactions with Customs online, prepared to go fully online once permitted, showcasing our commitment to leveraging technology for efficient service delivery.

Our FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

How does KMK Cargo handle customs clearance for different types of goods?

KMK Cargo specializes in custom clearance solutions for all types of goods, whether they arrive by air or sea, in bags, cartons, on pallets, or in containers. We offer tailored services based on the specific needs of your consignment, ensuring that your goods clear customs efficiently and swiftly. Our experienced team is well-versed in the latest policies and notifications, ensuring compliance and smooth clearance for various categories of cargo.

Can KMK Cargo assist with the transportation and storage of goods after customs clearance?

Yes, KMK Cargo provides comprehensive logistics solutions beyond customs clearance. After your goods clear customs, we can move them to any destination across the country, offering storage, break bulk, and distribution services as needed. Our aim is to provide a seamless, end-to-end service that meets all your logistics requirements, ensuring safe and timely delivery of your goods.

What makes KMK Cargo different from other logistics and customs clearance service providers?

What sets KMK Cargo apart is our comprehensive, integrated approach to logistics and customs clearance, tailored to meet the specific needs of each client. Our expertise in the field since 2016, combined with our commitment to innovation and technology, such as E-Filing and online transactions with Customs, positions us as market leaders. Our experienced team provides personalized attention to each consignment and seeks creative solutions to meet challenges, ensuring a professional, efficient, and timely service tailored to your requirements.
