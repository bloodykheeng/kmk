Our FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

What types of freight forwarding services does KMK offer?

KMK specializes in a wide range of freight forwarding services, including air and sea freight, customs clearance, warehousing, distribution, and domestic transportation. Our services are designed to accommodate the logistics needs of businesses of all sizes, ensuring efficient and reliable handling of goods from origin to destination.

How does KMK ensure the safety and security of the cargo?

Safety and security are paramount at KMK. We adhere to strict safety protocols throughout the transportation and warehousing process, including secure packaging, and safe handling practices. Our experienced team closely monitors each consignment to ensure its integrity until delivery.

Can KMK handle international shipments?

While our primary focus is on serving the logistics needs within Uganda, our network and partnerships allow us to facilitate international shipments. We work closely with reputable global logistics partners to ensure smooth and efficient international freight forwarding services.

How much do you charge for cargo?

The cost for transporting cargo with KMK Transport Wise Cargo Uganda Limited varies depending on several factors, including the type of goods, their weight, volume, destination, and specific service requirements such as storage or special handling. We recommend contacting us directly with your specific shipping details for an accurate quote.

How long does a cargo take?

The delivery time for cargo depends on the destination, the type of transportation used, and the specific service options chosen. KMK Transport is committed to providing timely and efficient delivery services tailored to meet customers' demands. For precise timing, please provide us with the details of your shipment.

What requirements are needed?

Shipping with KMK Transport requires details such as the nature and quantity of goods, preferred service (e.g., supply chain management, logistics, truck hire), destination, and any special handling or storage needs. For customs clearance and tax consultations, necessary documents might include invoices, packing lists, and any relevant permits or licenses. Contact us for a detailed list of requirements based on your specific cargo and services needed.
